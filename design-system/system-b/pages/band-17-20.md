# Band Override · Ages 17–20 · Young Adults (System B)
Overrides: system-b/MASTER.md Band 4 section

## Identity
Refined Minimalism — editorial print aesthetic on a dark digital canvas. Warm gold on near-black. Newsreader serif headlines carry gravitas. Inter body text maximises readability. This is news that feels worth sharing.

## Font Note
- Heading: **Newsreader** — elegant serif, italic at hero sizes brings print magazine energy
- Body: **Inter** — maximum legibility, familiar from every serious app
- Fallback chain: Newsreader → Georgia; Inter → DM Sans

## Active Overrides
- Background: `#111118` (near-black warm — premium, not gloomy)
- Card surface: `#1A1A24` (warm dark — blue-grey undertone)
- Raised surface: `#222230` (hover/active)
- Primary accent: `#C9A84C` deep gold (CTA, links, active, reading bar)
- Secondary accent: `#E8D5A3` pale gold (hover states)
- Headlines: warm gold-white `#F5E6C8`
- Body text: `#C8C8D8` cool grey
- Card borders: thin `1px rgba(201,168,76,0.15)` gold edge
- No pure black anywhere — minimum bg is `#111118`
- No sans-serif headings — Newsreader only for all display text
- No bounce/spring animations — smooth fade/slide only
